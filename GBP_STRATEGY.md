## 2.5. Service Area Business Setup Instructions

### How to Set Up as Service Area Business:

1. **When Creating/Editing Your Profile:**
   - Google will ask: "Do you want to add a location customers can visit?"
   - Select: **"No"** or **"I deliver goods and services to my customers"**
   - This will hide your address from public view

2. **Adding Service Areas:**
   - Go to: Info → Service areas
   - Add each city/town you serve:
     - Bath, Somerset
     - Bristol
     - Chippenham
     - Trowbridge
     - Frome
     - Bradford-on-Avon
     - Melksham
     - Warminster
     - Westbury
     - Corsham
     - Keynsham
     - Midsomer Norton
     - Radstock
     - Wells
     - Glastonbury
     - Shepton Mallet
     - Devizes
     - Calne
     - Swindon
   - You can add up to 20 service areas

3. **Verification Options for SAB:**
   - **Phone verification:** Google will call you with a code
   - **Video verification:** Google will verify via video call
   - **Postcard:** May still be required in some cases (sent to a private address)

4. **What Customers Will See:**
   - Instead of an address, they'll see: "Serves Bath, Bristol, Chippenham..." 
   - Your profile will still appear in local search results for all service areas
   - Map will show a general area rather than a specific pin

### Benefits of Service Area Business:
- ✅ No need to display your home/office address
- ✅ Can serve multiple cities/towns
- ✅ Still appears in local search for all service areas
- ✅ More professional for mobile/service businesses
- ✅ Protects privacy while maintaining local SEO benefits

4. **Regular Updates:** Post at least once per week
5. **Photo Quality:** Use high-resolution, well-lit photos
6. **Response Time:** Respond to reviews within 24-48 hours
7. **Authenticity:** Never fake reviews or use stock photos
8. **Local Focus:** Emphasize local service areas and community connection
